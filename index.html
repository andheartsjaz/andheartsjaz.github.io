Work

"Do They Like Me?"--An experimental project completed as a part of Thinkful's Data Science Immersive Program. Told from the perspective of a data scientist, we will test hypotheses:

  • "Has interest in Data Science increased or decreased?"
  • "Has interest in Data Science increased or decreased since the release of Harvard Business Review article, naming 'Data Scientist' the 'Sexiest job in the 21st Century'?"
    In this experimental build, we'll see just how popular the terms 'Data Science' and 'Data Scientist' are; and, test whether this popularity isstatistically significant. Project features original designs by researcher using Adobe Spark.

About

Jasmine is a CEO, Creative Director and Data Scientist from Chicago, IL. Known for her tenacity, openness and quirky personality, she pursued her career with sincerity and passion. Gifted with a strong will to succeed and deep yearning to understand the world, she first joined the US Navy before completing education in Philosophy and Civil Engineering. With certificates in Data Analytics and Project Management among with Fortune 500 business experience, she most recently completed intense scholarship in Data Science. She defines her work within the scope of data, design and engineering with subfocuses on economics, business intelligence and social science - however she prefers exploring the limits of programming, quantum mechanics and the fundamentals of calculus. In addition to her positioning in the data fields, she holds tenure as an creative insider for AdobeSpark, and technical insider and contributor for Unsplash—now in the Top 10% in her first year on the platform. She was named the TechExpert at BMW Technology, during Google’s Flutter International Hackathon. Jasmine is recognized by ESRI under their BlackGirlsMAPP initiative as GIS SME, Analyst and Data Scientist, the first in Illinois to receive this recognition. She creates under her business name—ViccarroCreative—where she has received invitations for nominations for the Timmy Awards and InterCon in 2019, contributed to Harvard Business Review and collaborates with esteemed Google Cloud Platform Developers. Additionally, her creative brand saw great success in 2019, capturing the top spots in nearly 100 key hashtags on multiple platforms. Shes captured the attention of notable fans including Team Trump, former Chicago Sun Times writer Bill Daley, Justin Timberlake, KKW Beauty, itCosmetics, Maybelline, The Duchess Meghan Markle, NewCastle United Defenders Fabian Schar and Federico Fernandez, the Bundesliga, the Premiere League, SportsTotal, Kswiss, McDonalds, the Swiss National Team, Gary Vee, Paul Scheer, Jeff Ross, John Luke of Storage Wars, The Glitch Mob, Djs Nick Monaco, Lee Burridge, and many more. She’s been a keynote speaker twice, holds medals from her military tours and is well known as a scholar in Philosophy, music, legal theory, psychology, economics, politics, theory of mind—to name a few. She humbly maintains her intense workload mostly from her iPhone screen, much to the surprise of her fans. Never deterred, she aims to become a major player in the tech and business world, pushing the limits of what we know is possible. When she’s not working or studying, she enjoys pinball, fitness, meditation, long walks and playing the many musical instruments of her knowledge base (including the voice). Jasmine has big dreams that she actively transfers to reality where others are always welcome with empathy, gentle wit and intention. Her favorite musician is Kanye West and her favorite food is Albanese sour gummy bears.

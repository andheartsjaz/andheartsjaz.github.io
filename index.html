Work

"Do They Like Me?"--An experimental project completed as a part of Thinkful's Data Science Immersive Program. Told from the perspective of a data scientist, we will test hypotheses:

  • "Has interest in Data Science increased or decreased?"
  • "Has interest in Data Science increased or decreased since the release of Harvard Business Review article, naming 'Data Scientist' the 'Sexiest job in the 21st Century'?"
    In this experimental build, we'll see just how popular the terms 'Data Science' and 'Data Scientist' are; and, test whether this popularity is statistically significant. Project features original designs by researcher using Adobe Spark.
    "Do they Like Me?" A/B Testing With Google's Keyword Searches

    Using the popular and informative US 'Adult' dataset--also known as "Census Income" dataset--we've turned to Supervised Learning, replicating curriculum methods, to see if we can predict a person's class/income status?
    Predicting Class/Income with Logistic Regression

    Here we will take a look into the respondents answering questions captured in the Multidimensional Sexual Self-Concept Questionnaire created in 1995 for general study of sexuality.
    Insights on Sexual Identity using Unsupervised Learning
